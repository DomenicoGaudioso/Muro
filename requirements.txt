  <h3>4.1 Verifica a Ribaltamento — NTC2018 §6.5.3.1.1 (Combinazione EQU)</h3>
  <p>Il polo di ribaltamento è posizionato al lembo estremo di valle (piede della fondazione). La combinazione EQU prevede γ<sub>dest</sub> = 1.1 e γ<sub>stab</sub> = 0.9.</p>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">FS Ribaltamento (NTC2018 §6.5.3 — Comb. EQU)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">FS<sub>rib</sub> = M<sub>stab</sub> / M<sub>dest</sub> ≥ 1.0</div>
  </div>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Momenti stabilizzanti (rispetto al piede — polo P)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">M<sub>stab</sub> = W<sub>cls</sub>·x<sub>cls</sub> + W<sub>terra</sub>·x<sub>terra</sub> + P<sub>p</sub>·z<sub>p</sub> + P<sub>v,a</sub>·B + M<sub>tirante</sub></div>
  </div>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Momento destabilizzante</div>
    <div class="formula-body">M<sub>dest</sub> = P<sub>a,h</sub>·(t<sub>base</sub> + H − z̄<sub>a</sub>) + F<sub>sis</sub>·y<sub>sis</sub></div>
  </div>

  <h3>4.2 Verifica a Scorrimento — NTC2018 §6.5.3.1.2 (Combinazione GEO)</h3>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">FS Scorrimento (Mohr-Coulomb interfaccia fondazione-terreno)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">FS<sub>scorr</sub> = (μ·V<sub>d</sub> + c<sub>u</sub>·B + P<sub>p</sub>) / P<sub>a,h</sub> ≥ 1.0</div>
  </div>

  <h3>4.3 Capacità Portante — Brinch-Hansen (NTC2018 §6.4.2.2)</h3>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Carico limite (formula trinomia di Brinch-Hansen)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">q<sub>lim</sub> = c·N<sub>c</sub>·i<sub>c</sub> + q·N<sub>q</sub>·i<sub>q</sub> + ½·γ·B'·N<sub>γ</sub>·i<sub>γ</sub></div>
  </div>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Fattori di capacità portante (Reissner-Prandtl)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">N<sub>q</sub> = e<sup>π·tan φ</sup>·tan²(45+φ/2) &nbsp;|&nbsp; N<sub>c</sub> = (N<sub>q</sub>−1)/tan φ &nbsp;|&nbsp; N<sub>γ</sub> = 2(N<sub>q</sub>+1)·tan φ</div>
  </div>

  <h3>4.4 Analisi Sismica — Mononobe-Okabe (NTC2018 §7.11.5)</h3>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Coefficiente di spinta attiva sismica (Mononobe-Okabe 1929)</div>
    <div class="formula-body">K<sub>a,MO</sub> = cos²(φ−θ) / [cos θ·cos(δ+θ)·(1+√(sin(φ+δ)·sin(φ−θ)/cos(δ+θ)))²] · (1−k<sub>v</sub>)</div>
  </div>
  <div class="formula-box">
    <div class="formula-label">Angolo di inclinazione risultante sismica</div>
    <div class="formula-body">θ = arctan(k<sub>h</sub> / (1−k<sub>v</sub>))</div>
  </div>
</section>
